Animations

Here are animations for my master's project, Nearly Isostatic Networks and Allosteric Effects. The write-up and source code are available here on github. The source pair (where the strain is applied across) is drawn in green, and the target is in red. Red dashed edges represent ghost bonds, which act as if they weren't there. I include the corresponding Figure in my project, where applicable.

Rocks et al. Fig. 1

The following animations are of our recreation of Fig. 1 from Rocks et al. The animations are:

  • Our solution for a strain ratio of 1, Fig. 6 a).
  • Their solution for a strain ratio of 1, Fig. 6 b).
  • Our solution (which is also their solution) for a strain ratio of -1.
  • The solution when using different pairs of sources and targets and aiming for a ratio of 1, Fig. 7.
